We Are Flowery Love

We are a small yet professional flower studio based in New York. Our mission is to make your lives brighter and beautiful, help you impress guests during important events, and make your loved ones happy with wonderful bouquets and flower arrangements through a trouble-free process. We work with freshly cut flowers to create elegant compositions.

Services

We offer a variety of floral services, from unique bouquets to event decoration.

Decoration

We create elegant flower decorations for homes, offices, shops, and other locations. Contact us if you want to demonstrate your style and taste to your guests, clients or visitors.

Floral Design

Our design team is always here to arrange unique and beautiful bouquets and flower compositions. Hurry up to surprise your loved ones and add some personal touch to your gifts.

Events

We know that details matter. That's why we provide and arrange flowers for private and corporate celebrations, charity galas, product launches, and other important events. Come to us to make your event an all-time memory.
